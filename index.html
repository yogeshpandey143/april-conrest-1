Beagle Breed Information

The Beagle is a breed of small hound that is similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. It is a scent hound, developed primarily for hunting hare.

Trivia

  1. The Beagle is a breed of small hound that is similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound.
  2. They were originally bred as scent hounds for tracking small game, such as rabbits and hares.
  3. The breed was recognized by the American Kennel Club in 1885 and is now one of the most popular breeds in the United States.
  4. Beagles are known for their friendly and curious personalities, and they make great family pets.
  5. Their keen sense of smell and high energy level make them excellent hunting companions and search and rescue dogs.
